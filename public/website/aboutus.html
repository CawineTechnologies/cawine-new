About Us

  • Cawine is the easiest, fastest, and most reliable way to order for Drinks, Alcohol, Eats & Cake and have it delivered within an hour. Cawine is Africa's largest Drinks, Eats, Alcohol & Cake marketplace and the best way to shop beer, wine, spirits, Eats and Cakes. Our customers trust us to be part of their lives – their celebrations, parties, dinners and quiet nights at home. We are there when it matters - committed to life's moments and the people who create them. We partner with the best retail stores and Cake shops in over 1,000 cities across Africa to serve up the best buying experience. Cawine offers a huge selection and competitive pricing with a side of personalized content. We're changing one of the largest, oldest and most resilient industries in the world using tech... and we love it. Each delivery promptly arrives at its destination within 30-60 minutes.
